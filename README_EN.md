# Knights of Honor II: Sovereign – 681 AD: First Bulgarian State

A historical mod for Knights of Honor II: Sovereign that adds a new starting scenario focused on the foundation of the First Bulgarian Empire.

This project takes players back to 681 AD, the year Khan Asparuh established Bulgaria in the Balkans after his victory over the Byzantine Empire.

---

## Features

- **New Starting Period**: 681 AD – First Bulgarian State.
- **Historical Accuracy**: Precisely defined realm borders across Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East.
- **Khanate of Bulgaria**: Bulgaria is accurately represented as a Khanate led by Khan Asparuh.
- **Full Localization**: Includes translations for territory names, titles, and ruler names in both English and Bulgarian.
- **Compatibility**: Designed to work seamlessly with the `BulgarianLangMod`.

---

## Installation

1. Navigate to your game's mod folder:
   `%USERPROFILE%\AppData\LocalLow\BlackSeaGames\Sovereign\Mods`

2. Copy the `681AD` folder into the `Mods` directory.

3. Launch the game.

4. Go to **Settings → Mods** and enable the `681AD` mod.

---

## Technical Information

- Scenario definitions are located in `Defs/CampaignVars.def`.
- Map and kingdom data can be found in `Maps/europe/`.
- Localization files are organized in `Texts/` (CSV format).
